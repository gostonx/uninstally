# Changelog

## 1.5.0 • July 9, 2026

This update introduces Recently Uninstalled, making it easier to keep track of apps you've removed and the storage you've recovered.

**New**

* Added Recently Uninstalled to the sidebar
* View your uninstall history, search and filter entries, and restore apps from the Trash when available
* See storage recovery statistics, including total space reclaimed and recently removed apps
* Added settings for managing and clearing uninstall history

---

## 1.4.5 • July 9, 2026

This update gives you more control over how apps are removed and brings a refreshed native macOS design.

**New**

* Choose whether apps are moved to the Trash or permanently deleted when uninstalling

**Improvements**

* Refreshed the interface with a more native macOS appearance
* Apps now disappear from the list immediately after uninstalling
* Added a link to Codenta in About

**Removed**

* Haptic feedback (use system-wide settings instead)

---

## 1.4.4 • July 9, 2026

This update improves navigation in Settings.

**Improvements**

* The Settings sidebar now always stays visible while browsing preferences

---

## 1.4.3 • July 9, 2026

This update makes managing Collections more convenient.

**New**

* Collections can now be renamed directly from the sidebar

## 1.4.2 • July 8, 2026

This update introduces Collections to help organize your apps.

**New**

* Create custom Collections to organize apps
* Drag and drop apps into Collections or add them from the context menu
* Rename, reorder, customize, and remove Collections

**Improvements**

* Sidebar customization is now accessible directly in the main window
